Cancelled Lunch

Earlier today, the school revealed in an announcement that the school lunch has been cancelled today due to increasingly bad weather. All students were informed that the catering company delivering food has not been able to drive their catering truck to the school due to heavy snowfall.

Our investigative team interviewed the school staff and none of them knew when this crisis would end. A survey was also conducted among staff and students where they were able to give feedback on whether the catering company should be kept or not. 85% voted for the catering company to be kept, 10% voted for a new catering company to be hired and 5% voted other.

This crisis is expected to negatively affect students' grades if it goes on for longer than a few days. Many students rely on the nutrients they get from the school lunch and therefore will be hungry during important events such as exams if the problem persists.

The lack of school lunch is only expected to last for approximately 2 days according to our investigative team, so there is no need to worry. If the problem persists past 2 days, the school has announced that they will offer pizza to all students from the money they have recieved from the refunds made by the catering company. This has recieved a majorly positive reaction from both students and teachers alike.

In conclusion there is no need to worry about the food shortage.
